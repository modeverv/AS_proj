R on Feeds Tree   Refreshes all feeds
M on Feeds Tree   Marks all items on feed/category as read
A on Feeds Tree   Marks all items on all feeds as read
ArrowUp
ArrowDown		   Moves up/down in Feeds Tree or in List View
ArrowRight
ArrowLeft		   Switches focus from Feeds Tree to List View or the other way round
Tab on article/preview   Switches focus from List View to Item Preview or the other way round
T on article   Opens the current article in a background loading tab
B on article   Opens the current article in browser
R on article   Refreshes the current feed
M on article   Marks all items in current feed as read
W in opened tab   Closes the opened tab
Double-Click on article   Opens the current article in browser
Del on category/feed   Deletes the current category / feed from the Tree
Double-Click on category   Quick rename of a category
