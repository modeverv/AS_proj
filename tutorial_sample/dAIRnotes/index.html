dAIRnotes is an HTML-based AIR sample application that allows several users to keep their diary notes on a computer.

The application leverages the popular dojo Javascript library, wich it takes to a drive test under the AIR platform. Namely, our application uses an arbitrary subset of dijits or dojo widgets, and three special storage provider classes that take advantage of AIR advanced storage abilities:

  • dojox.storage.AirEncryptedLocalStorageProvider
  • dojox.storage.AirDBStorageProvider
  • dojox.storage.AirFileStorageProvider
